Vanilla cupcakes

Fluffy, soft, and perfect for almost any occasion. They're made with simple ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and vanilla extract, often topped with a sweet buttercream or cream cheese frosting.

Apple Pie

Apple pie cake sounds delicious! It's a fusion between an apple pie and a cake, offering the warm, spiced flavor of apple pie in a soft, fluffy cake form. These cakes often have a spiced apple filling swirled inside a moist batter

Red velvet

Red velvet cake is a rich, moist, and slightly tangy cake that’s famous for its vibrant red color. The cake itself has a mild cocoa flavor, not too chocolaty, with a hint of vanilla. It's traditionally made with buttermilk, which gives it a subtle tang, and vinegar, which reacts with the cocoa to enhance the color.

Donuts

Donut doughnut is a deep-fried pastry, typically ring-shaped, though they can also come in filled forms. They’re crispy on the outside, soft and fluffy on the inside, and often coated in sugar or glazed with a sweet icing.

Cupcakes

A cupcake is a small, individual-sized cake that is typically baked in a paper or foil liner, making it easy to serve and enjoy. It’s often topped with a swirl of frosting and sometimes decorated with sprinkles, chocolate chips, or other fun toppings.
